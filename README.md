Sirui Sniper Series - Firmware Archive (Sony E-Mount)

This repository serves as a centralized library for firmware versions of the Sirui Sniper lens series (APS-C Autofocus) for Sony E-mount. The goal is to provide easy access to specific versions and maintain an update history for the 23mm, 33mm, and 56mm kit.

📦 Lens Lineup (Kit)

    23mm f/1.2

    33mm f/1.2

    56mm f/1.2

🛠 How to Update Firmware

The update process for Sirui Sniper lenses is done via the USB-C port located directly on the lens mount.

    Download: Choose the folder for your lens and download the latest firmware file.

    Connection: Connect the lens to your computer (Windows or macOS) using a high-quality USB-C cable.

    Recognition: The lens will appear on your system as a removable drive.

    Transfer: Copy the downloaded firmware file and paste it into the lens drive.

    Installation: * Wait for the copy to finish.

        Safely eject the drive from your OS.

        Disconnect the USB-C cable.

    Verification: Mount the lens on your camera and check the firmware version in the Sony settings menu (Menu > Setup > Version).

⚠️ Important Notices

    Proceed at your own risk. Ensure the USB cable is not disconnected during the transfer. I am not responsible for any failures during the update process.

    Compatibility: Always double-check that the file matches your lens's focal length.

    Official Source: These files are mirrored from the official Sirui website to ensure easier access for the filmmaking community.
